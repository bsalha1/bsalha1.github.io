ReliableSpigot Config Help

Non-World Settings

Field Default Meaning
tnt-optimized true Enables optimized TNT and explosion algorithms
redstone-optimized true Enables optimized redstone algorithms
clean-printer true Enables optimized Schematica printer algorithms
spawner.spawn-radius 4 How far the mobs can spawn away from the spawner
spawner.player-afk-radius 8 How far a player can stand near a spawner to work
spawner.need-players-nearby true If a player needs to be near a spawner for it to work
spawner.minimum-spawn-ticks 200 The least amount of ticks to spawn a mob from a spawner
spawner.maximum-spawn-ticks 800 The most amount of ticks to spawn a mob from a spawner
spawner.spawn-count 4 The amount of mobs to spawn per spawner activate
spawner.max-nearby-entities 6 The amount of entities that can be near in order for the spawner to work
spawner.tick-rate 1 How often the server ticks the spawner (increase to spawn faster)
config-version 1 The version of the config to use (this doesn't do anything)

PvP Settings

Field Default Meaning

verticle 0.4 Verticle knockback strength
verticle-max 0.4 The maximum strength of verticle knockback
horizontal 0.4 Verticle knockback strength
disable-sprint-knockback false Disables sprinting knockback
extra-sprint-knockback 0 Adds extra knockback while sprinting
unbreakable-armor false Toggle if armor should take durability damage
sword-armor-damage 2 The amount of damage a sword does to an armor's durability
axe-armor-damage 3 The amount of damage an axe does to an armor's durability
